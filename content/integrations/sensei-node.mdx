## Overview

Sensei Node is a professional blockchain infrastructure provider offering institutional-grade validator services and staking solutions. With a focus on security, reliability, and performance, Sensei Node enables institutions and individuals to participate in Lux network validation with confidence.

- **High Availability**: Redundant infrastructure for maximum uptime

- **Staking Services**: Comprehensive staking solutions for clients

- **Performance Monitoring**: Real-time validator performance tracking
